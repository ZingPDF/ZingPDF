Refund terms for ZingPDF subscriptions.

Renewals

Renewal charges are generally not refunded once processed. You can cancel at any time and your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

Exceptions

How to request a refund

Email tom@zingpdf.com with your order email, order reference if available, and a short explanation of the issue.
