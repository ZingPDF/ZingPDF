# ZingPDF Commercial Terms

Commercial licensing contact: Thomas Bowers, `tom@zingpdf.dev`

## 1. Commercial Model

ZingPDF is sold on a recurring subscription basis.

While a customer has an active paid subscription with sufficient licensed seats, that customer may use the Software commercially under `EULA.md`.

If a user or company is not using the Software for commercial gain, no paid commercial subscription is required.

## 2. Standard License Offers

Current standard license offers are commercial packaging and sales labels only and may change over time. They are not hard-coded license rights inside the Software.

Current labels are:

- Solo
- Team
- Enterprise

The exact seat count, support entitlement, pricing, and service model for each offer may change over time and are controlled by the applicable checkout page, quote, order, or invoice.

## 3. Seat Coverage

Customers must hold enough paid seats for the individuals using the Software on behalf of that same legal entity.

Contractors may use seats licensed to the contracting legal entity.

Each separate legal entity must maintain its own license coverage unless Licensor agrees otherwise in writing.

## 4. Included Packages

Unless Licensor states otherwise in writing, all paid subscriptions include access to all generally offered ZingPDF packages.

This includes:

- `ZingPDF`
- `ZingPDF.GoogleFonts`
- `ZingPDF.FromHTML`

## 5. Bundling

Bundling the unmodified Software inside a customer's own application is permitted during an active paid subscription, provided that:

- required licensing assets, terms, notices, and attribution remain intact
- the customer does not present ZingPDF itself as a standalone library, SDK, package, service, or competing product

## 6. Resale Restriction

Reselling ZingPDF itself is not permitted unless Licensor expressly agrees otherwise in writing.

This restriction is intended to prevent third parties from repackaging or competing with ZingPDF directly while still allowing customers to use ZingPDF inside their own products.

## 7. Cancellation And Refunds

Subscriptions may be cancelled at any time.

Unless required by law:

- no refunds are provided
- cancellation stops future renewals only
- existing subscription coverage remains active until the end of the already-paid billing cycle

## 8. Evaluation And Non-Commercial Use

Non-commercial use and evaluation use are governed by `EVALUATION_TERMS.md`.

Commercial use begins when the Software is used for commercial gain or business operations outside genuine evaluation.
